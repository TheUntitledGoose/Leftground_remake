What are WiFi resrictions

Wifi restrictions are restrictions that, rather than being enforced by and extention or a managed computer, are enfored by the wifi/network that the chromebook is connected too.

VPNs

A VPN, (virtual private network), establishes a digital connection between your computer and a remote server owned by a VPN provider, creating a point-to-point tunnel that encrypts your personal data, masks your IP address, and lets you sidestep website blocks and firewalls on the internet.

How to use VPN:

  • Download working VPN
  • Go to chrome://network
  • Scroll down and find “Import ONC File”, under it you should see “Choose File”
  • Add your VPN file
  • Go to your shelf and click “VPN” (if it's not there you may have to do chrome://restart)
  • Find your VPN and connect to it
  • Network restrictions should be bypassed
